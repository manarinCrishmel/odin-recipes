Toast Recipe

Description

Ah, toast, the pinnacle of culinary innovation. Who needs fancy toppings or mouthwatering flavors when you can experience the sheer delight of eating dry, toasted bread? The way it crumbles in your mouth, leaving behind a satisfying feeling of emptiness, truly makes it a gastronomic delight. Why bother with butter or jam when you can revel in the simple pleasure of plain, unadulterated toast? It's the perfect dish for those who have an unyielding desire to embrace the most basic and uninspiring culinary experiences. So grab your knife, slice that bread, and prepare to be underwhelmed by the magic of plain toast. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Bread Sliced
  • Butter optional

Directions

  1. Take piece of bread in right or left hand.
  2. With alternate hand, open toaster- NOTE: This step may vary depending on your toaster oven.
  3. Place piece of bread in toaster oven.
  4. Turn dial/knob determine the level of darkness to your desire [Assuming your toaster oven is plugged into a working electric socket]
  5. Turn on toaster
  6. Wait.
  7. Don protective gear.
  8. Remove toast (no longer bread) from toaster over when the toasting of said toast is no longer toasting.
